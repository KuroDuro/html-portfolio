Best Movies List

THE TOP 3 I CAN REMEMBER Edition

Gunndam Wing Endless Waltz

This is movie from my childhood that really got me into the Gunndam Universe. It also takes place during christmas which technically makes it a perfect holiday film.

Shin Kamen Rider ( 2023)

This movie recently came out and what a treat it was. Based off the 1992 Original named the same. We see our protagonist Takeshi Hongo's Vigilant battles against the evil SHOCKER. Everything in this movie made you feel like you were back in the 90"s watching a action movie

5 Cenimeters Per Second

This Animated movie is Makotro Shinkai. The movie takes place between the earlier 1990's - 2008 about the love between Takaki Tono and Akari Shinohara. The movie is told in 3 beautiful written parts a definite must watch.
